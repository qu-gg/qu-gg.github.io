Introduction

Written/Adapted & Edited by Quagg
Many adventures adapted from One-Shot Wonders by Roll-N-Play Press
Hi there,

Welcome to The Helical Expeditions! This is a collection of ready-to-use one-shot adventures set within the TTRPG BREAK!!. I made this collection to show off the diversity of adventures and themes one can have within its lore, and to have a set for myself to run. Each adventure represents my attempt to connect deeper with the setting and have fun getting lost in imagining what's there and what stories come and go.

BREAK!! is the first system I've really felt comfortable breaking apart and crafting my own stories in. It's such an endearing setting that you can put anything into and have it mesh seamlessly. I've run it weekly for nearly 3 years now, and have run many cherished adventures in it.

I owe a lot to the BREAK!! community for the support they've given around my creations (and tools!).

Whether you're just looking for fresh material or are new to the Outer World entirely, I hope these expeditions spark joy and inspire memorable sessions at your table as they have mine.

Enjoy!

— Quagg

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions
Does every adventure include all aspects of BREAK!! rules (Journey, Negotiation, Combat)?
No, and intentionally so. Each adventure's set of rules used is molded around the scope of the adventure. Many adventures can be completed without violence at all. Some focus heavily on exploration and discovery, others on social intrigue, and some on fighting wondrous foes in wondrous locations.
Are the locations/lore presented within here canon to the universe?
No. However, many of the locations and lore were inspired by the BREAK!! corebook and blogposts (even early 2014 posts!). Regardless, they are not representative of official lore coming down the line.
How many Player Characters is this designed for?
Roughly 3-4. However, with BREAK!!, it is fairly easy to adjust. Throw in some Mooks/Bosses, reduce some stats here or there. Play by ear, but I find you can often go harder than you think on characters!
What are your inspirations?
Too many. While many of the story-beats themselves stem from One-Shot Wonders by Roll-N-Play Press, the re-flavor inspirations stem from my favorite media. To name a few: BREAK!!, She-Ra, Final Fantasy, Ghibli, Adventure Time, Gravity Falls, Avatar: The Last Airbender, Nier, Moebius, the OSR Community, and the list goes on and on. I hope the whimsy and imagination of these amazing works peek through in this work.
Use of Generative AI?
No generative AI was used in writing or considered for art sources - just human brainwaves there. Standard coding agents were used to help with some of the more funky aspects of front-end formatting and development. All creative content and artistic direction came from good old-fashioned imagination and many hours of tinkering.
