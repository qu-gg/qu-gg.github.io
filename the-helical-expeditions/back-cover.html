X+ expeditions spanning the Outer World - from the mournful shadows
of Wistful Dark to the war-torn depths of the Buried Kingdom. Each expedition is a one-session story, filled with colorful characters, wondrous locations, worldbuilding, and engaging adversaries waiting to challenge your group.
Each are within 3 pages or less for quick-reading, quick-running.

Whether your adventurers are protecting endangered Prism Rokko-Dos from poachers, racing Skimmers through craggy canyons, or even flying with
sky whales, The Helical Expeditions has a journey for every table.
